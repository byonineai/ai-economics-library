# **AI Economics Library**

A curated collection of books, research papers, and notes exploring the intersection of **economics and artificial intelligence**.

Artificial intelligence is transforming markets, labor, and institutions. Algorithms influence decisions in finance, hiring, healthcare, and public policy. Understanding these transformations requires not only technical knowledge, but also economic insight.

**This repository gathers literature and ideas that help explain how ** **AI reshapes economic systems, labor markets, productivity, inequality, and the future of work** **.**


# **Purpose**

The goal of this repository is to create a personal and academic library that explores:

* **The ****economics of artificial intelligence**
* **Automation and labor markets**
* **Productivity and technological change**
* **Economic inequality in the age of AI**
* **Digital platforms and data economies**
* **AI governance and policy**
